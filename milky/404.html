Our Essence

The Legacy of Sonai highlighted below

Vision

To build the most technologically advanced and ecologically efficient dairy operations, ensuring the best value for money for every stakeholder. We prioritize animal health and consumer nutrition to deliver excellence.

Our Strength

India’s top FMCG brands trust Sonai Milk and Milk Products. This trust reflects our unwavering commitment to quality, which begins at the grassroots level and extends to every product we offer.

History

Since 2002, Sonai Dairy has been committed to delivering milk in its purest form. Guided by the visionary Shri Dashrath Mane, we’ve embraced technology to eliminate contamination and uplift the rural economy.

Achievements

From processing 12,000 liters to 30 lakh liters of milk daily, Sonai’s journey is a testament to hard work, vision, and dedication, making it an internationally recognized brand.

Technology & Growth

Our focus on 100% technology integration has ensured the highest quality products. Investments in infrastructure, training, and rural development have fostered sustainable growth and transformed the dairy industry.
